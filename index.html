PropView Privacy Policy

PropView is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal information when you use PropView. Your privacy matters to us so whether you are new to PropView or a longtime user, please do take the time to get to know our practices – and if you have any questions contact us at propviewassist@gmail.com or through the app.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from basic stuff like which language you speak, to more sensitive information like your device information.

We collect information in the following ways:

 -> Information we get from your use of our services. We collect information about errors and crashes encountered while using PropView. This allows us to improve the functionality and usability of the app. Some of the log information we collect is:

-> Device information

We collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and network information).

-> Log information

When you use our services or view content provided by PropView, we automatically collect and store certain information in your device logs. This includes device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, your device date, time and locale.

-> Location information

When you use PropView, we may collect and process information about your actual location. We use GPS to determine location for use in the map functionality in PropView. Your location is never transmitted off the device nor shared outside of PropView.

-> Unique application numbers

Our services include a unique application number. This number and information about your installation (for example, the operating system type and application version number) may be sent to PropView when you install or uninstall that service or when that service periodically contacts our servers, such as for automatic updates.

-> Local storage

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device.

How we use information we collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve PropView and our users.

When you contact PropView, we keep a record of your communication, device and log information to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements, or reply to you when you contact us through PropView.

We use information collected from other technologies, to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. For example, by saving your language preferences, we’ll be able to have our services appear in the language you prefer. Or by saving your view settings, PropView is able to launch with your preferred view settings already loaded.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of PropView unless one of the following circumstances applies:

-> With your consent

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of PropView when we have your consent to do so.

-> For external processing

We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

-> For legal reasons

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of PropView if we have a goodfaith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

-> meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request, enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations. detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.

-> protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of PropView, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

We may share non-personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners – like publishers, advertisers or connected sites. For example, we may share information publicly to show trends about the general use of our services.

If PropView is involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes). We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.
