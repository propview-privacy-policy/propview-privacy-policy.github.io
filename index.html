Privacy Policy

There are many different ways you can use our services – to add tasks onto your list, set reminders for upcoming tasks, add your current location to your task. When you share information with us, for example by creating a TaskTracker account, we use that information to make our services available to you– this allows you to share a task with people, or set a task to your current location. As you use our services, we want to be clear how we’re using this information and the ways in which you can protect your privacy.

Our Privacy Policy explains:

What information we collect and why we collect it. How we use that information.

 

Your privacy matters to us so whether you are new to TaskTracker or a longtime user, please do take the time to get to know our practices – and if you have any questions contact us at absataylor92@gmail.com or through the app.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from basic stuff like which language you speak, to more sensitive information like your email and passwords.

We collect information in the following ways:

 Information you give us. TaskTracker requires you to have a user account in order to use our services. When you do, we’ll ask for personal information, like your name, email address, and password to store with your account. When creating a task and setting the location feature, TaskTracker may ask for permission to retrieve your current location. If you decide to set a reminder for your task, we will ask for permission to access your calendar and reminders.

 Information we get from your use of our services. We collect information about errors and crashes encountered while using TaskTracker. This allows us to improve the functionality and usability of the app. Some of the log information we collect is:

Device information

We collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information). TaskTracker may associate this information with your user account.

Log information

When you use our services or view content provided by TaskTracker, we automatically collect and store certain information in server logs. This includes device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, your device date, time and locale.

Location information

When you use TaskTracker, we may collect and process information about your actual location. We use GPS to determine location.

Unique application numbers

Our services include a unique application number. This number and information about your installation (for example, the operating system type and application version number) may be sent to TaskTracker when you install or uninstall that service or when that service periodically contacts our servers, such as for automatic updates.

 Local storage

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device.

How we use information we collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect TaskTracker and our users. 

We may use the name you provide for your TaskTracker Profile across all of the services we offer. If other users already have your email, or other information that identifies you, we may show them your publicly visible username. Users are able to send tasks to you if they have your username or email used to register your TaskTracker account. 

When you contact TaskTracker, we keep a record of your communication and device information to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements, or reply to you when you contact us through TaskTracker.

We use information collected from other technologies, like to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. For example, by saving your language preferences, we’ll be able to have our services appear in the language you prefer. Or by saving your view settings, TaskTracker is able to launch with your preferred view settings already loaded.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

Accessing and updating your personal information

Whenever you use our services, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information. If that information is wrong, we strive to give you ways to update it quickly or to delete it – unless we have to keep that information for legitimate business or legal purposes.

We aim to maintain our services in a manner that protects information from accidental or malicious destruction. Because of this, after you delete information from our services, we may not immediately delete residual copies from our active servers and may not remove information from our backup systems.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of TaskTracker unless one of the following circumstances applies:

With your consent

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of TaskTracker when we have your consent to do so.

For external processing

We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

For legal reasons

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of TaskTracker if we have a goodfaith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request. enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations. detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.

protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of TaskTracker, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

We may share non-personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners – like publishers, advertisers or connected sites. For example, we may share information publicly to show trends about the general use of our services.

If TaskTracker is involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes). We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.
